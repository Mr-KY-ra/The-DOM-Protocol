## üöÄ Key Pillars of the System

### 1. Proof-of-Merit (PoM)
Legislative authority is not granted by popularity, but by capability. Candidates must pass an advanced **Analytical Proficiency Exam** to qualify for the assembly. Only the top performers are eligible to join the 10,000-member body.

### 2. Asymmetric Anonymity
The identity of the 10,000 legislators remains completely hidden from the Executive branch (the Government). This renders lobbying, bribery, and political pressure technically impossible. The government proposes; the "Invisible Ten Thousand" decide.

### 3. Biometric Escrow & Privacy
Votes are cast using local-only biometric verification. Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP) ensure "One-Human, One-Vote" integrity without storing sensitive personal data on central servers.

### 4. Smart-Contract Legislation
Approved laws and budget allocations are executed via immutable smart contracts. Once the DOM reaches a 51% consensus, the decision is irreversible and transparently recorded on the ledger.
